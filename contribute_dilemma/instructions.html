Over the next 10 rounds, you will be grouped randomly and anonymously with another {{ C.NUM_OTHER_PLAYERS }} participants.
In each round, you will be randomly assigned a cost that ranges uniformly from 0 to 120.
Each of you decides independently and simultaneously whether you will contribute or not.

How earnings are determined

  • If at least {{C.NUMCONS}} members in your group contribute, everyone will get {{ C.GENERAL_BENEFIT }}.
  • If fewer than {{C.NUMCONS}} members contribute, everyone receives nothing.
  • However, you will pay your cost if you contribute, regardless of if the threshold is met.

Note that your earnings each round will depend on both your own choice and the choices of other group members.

How you will be paid off

  • Your earnings will be accumulated until the end of the experiment.
  • Each point you earn is equivalent of $0.01. However, if your total earnings are negative points at the end of this experiment, your earnings will be converted to $0.
  • You will be paid off your earnings plus participation fee ($3) when you leave the room. Your payoff from this experiment will range between $0 ~ $23.
